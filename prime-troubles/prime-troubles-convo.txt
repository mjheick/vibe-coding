Accepted creation of

Now you'll need a linker config for the NES ROM layout:

Accepted creation of

nes.cfg


prime-troubles-nes.asm and nes.cfg are ready. Build with the cc65 toolchain:

ca65 prime-troubles-nes.asm -o prime.o
ld65 prime.o -C nes.cfg -o prime-troubles.nes
Then load prime-troubles.nes in an emulator (FCEUX, Mesen, etc.) or burn it to a cartridge.

What's happening under the hood:

iNES header declares NROM mapper 0 with 32KB PRG and CHR-RAM
On reset: initializes PPU, uploads a bitmap font (0–9, A–Z) into CHR-RAM pattern table, sets a black/white palette, clears the nametable
Runs the same prime + formula logic, writes each matching number as tile indices directly to the PPU nametable at $2000
Results display in rows of 5 across the screen, then enables rendering and halts in an infinite loop
