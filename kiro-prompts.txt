lunar-lander:
- We're going to create a spaceship lander game in a browser window
- The lander will be shown as a white square with a silver triangle top and two protruding legs
- The game field will be a black background with random stars (about 5% full) and a white flat lunar ground take takes 1% of the screen space.
- The lander will fall from the top at a rate approaching the white flat lunar ground
- The fall speed will be calculated at the gravitational rate of the moon, which is 1.625 meters per second squared
- The lander can "fire it's engines" by hitting the spacebar. When the engines fire it will counter the gravitational fall and slow the descent of the lander.
- If the engines stop firing the lunar gravity will pull the lander faster to the ground.
- The engines consume fuel at a rate of 1 per second, and the lander has 50 fuel to start.
- The game starts by hitting the letter G and shows the lander at the top and the game field
- There needs to be a top banner with the text "Lunar Lander"
- There needs to be basic instructions at the bottom, stating what button to start the game and what button to engage the engines.
- There needs to be a scoreboard on the side showing the height (in meters) the lunar lander is from the lunar surface, the rate of descent (as a positive integer), and how much fuel is left.
- If the lander hits the surface where the descent rate is less than 1.0 then it's a safe landing, otherwise it's a crash.
- If there is crash the words "CRASH" in red show up in the middle of the game field
- If there is a safe landing the words "SAFE" show up in green in the middle of the game field.
- Hitting G starts a new game, and this needs to show up under "CRASH" or "SAFE" in white letters under the middle of the game field.
